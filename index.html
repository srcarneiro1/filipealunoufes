I just remembered that I love eating ramen noodles, even if it's not so good for health. For that reason, I'm going to share a recipe for you to make:

ON MICROWAVE

1) Place 400 ml of water and the noodles in a microwave-safe container;

2) Cook for 5 minutes*, stopping halfway through to stir (use your head and calculate);

3) Remove from the microwave, mix the seasoning and serve!

* The preparation time may vary according to the model, power and time of use of the microwave. If you prefer, return the product to the microwave until it is to your liking. Preparation method defined based on 800W microwave.

ON THE STOVE

1) Boil 400 ml of water;

2) Add the noodles and cook for about 3 minutes;

3) Remove from the fire, put the seasoning in the envelope and mix well!
